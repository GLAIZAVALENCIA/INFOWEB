    • Public Speaking

    • Time Management

  • Time management is something I constantly strive to improve in my daily life. I find that when I plan my day ahead, I’m more productive and less stressed. I start by prioritizing tasks that are most important or have strict deadlines. I try to break down larger projects into smaller, manageable steps, which helps me stay focused and avoid feeling overwhelmed. Occasionally, distractions can throw me off track, but I’m learning to set boundaries, such as limiting social media during work hours. I also allocate time for short breaks to recharge, which helps me stay fresh throughout the day. Sometimes, things don’t go as planned, and I remind myself to be flexible and adapt. Balancing work, study, and personal time can be tricky, but I believe consistency is key. I try to reflect at the end of the day to see what worked well and what I can improve. By managing my time better, I feel more in control of my schedule and my goals.
